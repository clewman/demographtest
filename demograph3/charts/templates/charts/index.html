Citizen Centric, Data Democratized

Demograph is a web application that allows you to create graphs that show the disparity between income, education, and gender in the United States over a five year period. The data comes from the US Census API, American Community Survey (ACS), which collects information for US counties with populations of 65,000 or more. You can view by county, state, or with comparison charts.

There are several graph options: county and state choropleths, bar charts, scatterplots, and line graphs. You can create county and state choropleths based on gender, income level, year (2012-2017), and/or education level. You can also create a line chart, scatter plot, or bar graph displaying comparisons of men and women, both above and below the poverty level.
